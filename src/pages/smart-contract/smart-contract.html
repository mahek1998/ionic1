Smart Contract

The contract is between the Quick Deals suppliers and customers with the primary purpose of a transparent and sincere comm-erical transaction. The peer to peer facilitationwill by pass the intermediaried and reduce timeand cost. The outline below provides an under-standing of the deliverables and the commit-ments of the related parties.

Supplier

1. The supplier to sign a contract with the predetermined requirment to support the value proposition and prevent the supplier from retracting the content and conditions of the contract during the sales period.

2. Sales Period : is when a product or service is placed on the Quick. Deals website over a 14-day sales period with limited inventory

3. Inventory : The limited inventory is placed on the website and cannot be retracted during the sales period. Limited inventory is subjected to the 14 days sales period or depletion of inventory.

4. Payment settlement in full at the end of the sales period and the product or service tansferred to the customers

5. Predetermined incremental discount rates are confirmed before the sales period and tiered at 3 or 4 levels based on the volume inventory sold.

6. Based on the contract the payment settlement to the supplier will be suhjected to the valume of inventory sold and the final discount rate achieved at the end of the sale.

Customer

1. Customer will be able to buy the product or service during the Sales period.

2. Payment for the product and service will be made at the point of purchase pending the completion of sales period or depletion of inventory.

3. Customer will received a 5% cash rebate upon purchase of products or services. Collectable at the end of the sale period.

4. All items for sale will include a value add proposition attached to the product or service during the sales period.

5. Customers will enjoy incremental cash rebate based on a specific product or service that the customer buys and the volume of inventory sold based the diffident tiers.

6. At the end of the sales period based on the valume of a specific product or service sold the customer that participated in the purchase will receive a final cash rebate.

7. The sales fullfillment is at the end of the sales period of 14 days or the depletion of inventory. Whichever is first.
